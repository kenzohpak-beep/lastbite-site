Our Mission

Reduce food waste by making surplus affordable and convenient

Perfectly good food is thrown away every day at closing time. LastBite helps local businesses sell surplus at deep discounts, so customers get affordable meals and communities generate less waste.

How we create impact

  • Rescue surplus: unsold food is redirected to customers instead of the bin.
  • Make food affordable: students, families, and workers get real meals at lower cost.
  • Support local businesses: partners earn incremental revenue and gain sustainability visibility.
  • Reduce emissions: less wasted food means fewer embedded emissions are wasted.
  • Give back: 5% of LastBite profits are donated to the UN World Food Programme (WFP).

What makes LastBite different

  • Not a full-menu delivery app: only end-of-day surplus listings.
  • Off-peak operations: closing-time clustering helps reduce operational cost.
  • Transparent value: each listing shows discount versus original value.
  • Built-in tracking: savings and impact update after you order.
