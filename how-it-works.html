How It Works

A surplus-only flow built for closing time

LastBite is focused on end-of-day surplus, not full-price menus. That means deeper discounts, less waste, and a smoother operational flow for partners at closing.

1) Partners list surplus

Near closing, partners list surplus items that are still great to eat — at 40–70% off.

2) You reserve a pickup window

Browse deals by partner and category, add to cart, and select pickup (or delivery when available).

3) Track savings + impact

After you order, LastBite tracks your estimated food saved, CO₂ avoided, and your savings.

Why closing-time only?

  • Better discounts: surplus is priced to move before closing.
  • Lower costs: deliveries can be batched near end-of-day.
  • Less waste: surplus gets eaten instead of thrown out.
  • Extra revenue for partners: turning waste into income.

Common questions

What kind of items are listed?

Typically baked goods, desserts, bagels, and late-night slices—whatever is left at closing.

Do I know exactly what I’m getting?

Some deals are “surprise bags” and others are specific items. Each deal listing shows what to expect.

How do you calculate impact?

We estimate impact per meal rescued using standard factors (food weight and CO₂e). You can see these factors in the Impact Tracker.
