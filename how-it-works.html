How It Works

A surplus-only flow built for closing time.

Partners list surplus near closing. Customers reserve through LastBite. Orders cluster when kitchens and drivers are less busy — keeping fees lower.

1) Partners list surplus

Restaurants, cafés and shops post end-of-day items at 40–70% off.

2) You reserve

Browse by category and dietary needs. Reserve for a timed pickup window (or delivery where offered).

3) Track impact

See estimated meals rescued, food saved, CO₂ avoided — plus your savings.

Pricing (prototype)

Typical discount range: 40–70% off the original menu value.

Pickup: higher margin (no delivery cost)
Delivery: available for select partners

This demo uses simple assumptions for impact and donation estimates.

FAQ (prototype)

Are these items safe?

Partners only list items that are unsold but still good to eat. Always follow allergy notes.

What if I miss the pickup window?

Pickup windows are short. In a real launch, missed windows may be non-refundable.

How is impact calculated?

This prototype uses simple estimates per meal. Replace with verified methodology at launch.
