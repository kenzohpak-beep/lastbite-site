How LastBite works

A simple way to rescue good food that would otherwise be wasted.

1
Browse surplus deals

Partners list end-of-day surplus near closing. Deals are clearly priced with the original value shown.

2
Choose pickup or delivery

Add items to your cart and select pickup or delivery (where available). Pickup windows are shown per item.

3
Rescue food & track impact

Every order contributes to food-waste reduction. Your Impact Tracker shows meals rescued, food saved, and CO₂e avoided.

Why closing-time matters

This is the lowest-friction moment to rescue surplus.

  • Kitchen output is already complete for the day.
  • Surplus is known, so listings are accurate.
  • Pickup windows are short and predictable.
  • Delivery batching is easier off-peak.

For partners

Turn surplus into revenue with minimal effort.

Partner benefits
  • Incremental revenue from surplus items that would be discarded.
  • Less waste and fewer disposal costs.
  • Brand lift by participating in a waste-reduction program.
  • Operationally simple: listings are clustered at closing time.
How listing works
  1. Set a pickup window and surplus quantity.
  2. Choose discount level (typical 40–70%).
  3. Orders are confirmed and paid in-app.
  4. Hand off at pickup, or to LastBite delivery in the same window.

  • Donations: 5% of profits support the UN World Food Programme (WFP).
