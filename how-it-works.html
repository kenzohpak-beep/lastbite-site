How LastBite works

A simple way to rescue good food that would otherwise be wasted.

1
Browse surplus deals

Partners list end-of-day surplus near closing. Deals are clearly priced with the original value shown.

2
Choose pickup or delivery

Add items to your cart and select pickup or delivery (where available). Pickup windows are shown per item.

3
Rescue food & track impact

Every order contributes to food-waste reduction. Your Impact Tracker shows meals rescued, food saved, and CO₂e avoided.

Pricing
  • Discounts: typically 40–70% off original value.
  • Pickup: lower cost; no driver fee.
  • Delivery: available on select deals; priced to stay affordable during off-peak.
  • Donations: 5% of profits support the UN World Food Programme (WFP).
